Accommodations

Rooms at the Hilton Garden Inn Issaquah are available for guests to book any time from September 2-5. Please use this link or Group Code 619 to book a discounted room in our reserved block.

If you would like to book outside of September 2-5, make your reservation by calling the hotel directly at (425) 837-3600. While you won't be able to get our discounted rate outside of those dates, they will add a note to your reservation you don't have to switch rooms.

Shuttle

A complimentary shuttle service will be provided throughout the wedding running between the Hilton Garden Inn and venue starting an hour before the ceremony.

Parking

The Pickering Barn has a parking lot on the south side of the venue with 145 parking stalls. Handicapped and carpool stalls are provided. Parking is complimentary, but overnight parking is not permitted.
